    ABOUT US



    Adam and Eve motors limited is an indigenous registered
    transportation company with its head office at No 50A Ngwa road
    Aba, Abia State. The company which is now fully into public mass
    transport business was started by late Chief John Okoye in the early
    eighties.



    THE OBJECTIVES - ADAM & EVE MOTORS




    To make the movement of passengers and goods easy for the
    public.


    To help in alleviating the transportation problems in the
    country.To help in alleviating the transportation problems in the
    country.


    To provide easy channel for people of Niger Delta region have
    access to all the geo- political Zones of the country.


    To work towards being one of the best mass transit companies
    in Nigeria.


    To contribute to the economic development of Nigeria through
    provision of affordable transportation system.


    To partner with the Government in the creation of employment
    opportunities with interest of the people of the Niger Delta
    region.



    OUR SERVICIES


    The company's main product and services constitute mainly of mass

    transportation of passengers and goods to and from different cities in Nigeria.
    We offer unmatched pleasant and relaxing travel experience. You are assured
    that your safety, comfort and other value-added service is guaranteed. Bellow

    are other transportation services that we offer.


    Vehicle Charter/Hiring Service


    Our charter service at Adam and Eve Motors makes our fleet yours for a defined period time at a very affordable price which makes you travel like an elite from your desired location to your choice destination.

    Inter-City Transport Service


    Our Inter-City Transportation Service is available in the following routes: Aba, Umuahia, Amawbia, Ekwulobia, Uyo, Calabar and Onitsha. You can travel in a reliable, cosy interiors and fitted with stereo entertainment vehicle.

    Haulage


    Adam and Eve Motors operate several modern fleet of standard and reliable vehicles dedicated to our haulage services, so you have complete peace of mind that we are totally professional in this business.

    Courier & Cargo Delivery


    In this category, Adam & Eve Motors assures deliveries of consignments in various cities. This provision is custom-made for the seamless delivery of urgent consignments like delivery of documents and parcels.
